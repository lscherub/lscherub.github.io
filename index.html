about me.

i'm a digital designer in love with art, culture, and discovering new worlds — online and offine. i enjoy crafting clean, beautiful interfaces and building digital spaces that feel easy to use and fun to explore.
my creative process often starts with writing strange little pieces of code or obsessing over layout details until everything clicks.

over the last 5+ years, i've worked across e-commerce, wellness, and furniture brands, blending marketing, design, and operations to help small teams do big things. i've built and scaled Shopify stores, improved customer retention with email and popups, and even cut ad costs by 58% — all while keeping things creative and human.

outside of work, you'll usually find me on a side project, messing with sound design, taking photos of light and shadows, or reading poetry i'll never post. i also browse way too much reddit.

got a project in mind? say "Hi!" — i'd love to collaborate.
